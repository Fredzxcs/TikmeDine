About Us

In the heart of a hustling and bustling city, THE ROYALCARE VENUE is a Sanctuary of relaxation, safety, and peace.
Along with the Royal Café and Tikme Dine, We pride ourselves on our high-class provincial vibe designed to cater for your occasion. We provide not just delightful and sumptuous FEASTS, but also memories worthy OF Remembering for a lifetime.

Our Cozy Indoor Dining Space

Step into a warm and inviting atmosphere at Tikme Dine...

Serenity Awaits Outdoors

Surrounded by lush greenery, the exterior of Tikme Dine is a picturesque escape from the everyday. Ascend our charming staircase, lined with vibrant purple railings, to discover an inviting terrace that overlooks our beautifully manicured garden. The exterior design, inspired by classic Filipino architecture with modern twists, features a serene white facade adorned with climbing plants and a golden crown emblem. Nestled among palms and tropical plants, this tranquil space sets the tone for a dining experience that feels like a retreat into nature—whether you’re dining in the garden or inside, the ambiance promises relaxation and delight.

Serene Garden Terrace

Step into a tranquil oasis with our serene garden terrace, where nature and comfort combine to create the perfect dining atmosphere. The natural beauty of lush greenery surrounds the space, offering a peaceful backdrop as you dine at the elegantly designed wooden table and wicker chairs. Overhead, a sophisticated green canopy provides the ideal amount of shade, making the terrace enjoyable in any weather. The contemporary design of the tiled flooring enhances the calm ambiance, while the glass railing offers a seamless view of the surrounding garden. Whether you're hosting a gathering or seeking a quiet meal, our garden terrace ensures a refreshing and memorable experience.
